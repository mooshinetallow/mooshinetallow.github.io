About

Welcome!

My name is Claudia Ruhenkamp, and I am a stay-at-home momma. I began the hobby of making beef tallow moisturizers for my family and friends and was amazed by the benefits and results that came, so I decided to share with the community!

Tallow is bio-identical to our skin's oils, making it easily absorbable without clogging pores. It is deeply nourishing and moisturizing, is packed full of vitamins and minerals, and has anti-bacterial, anti-microbial, anti-inflammatory, and anti-aging properties. Alongside the other skin-healthy ingredients, you are sure to benefit and love using it for yourself and your family!

This all-natural formula has many benefits and uses including use as a face cream, body lotion, treatment for dry or irritated skin, healing eczema, decreasing baby diaper rash, reducing/preventing stretch marks, and more!
